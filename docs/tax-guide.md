# Canadian Tax Guide for Creators

## GST/HST Basics

| Province | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Ontario | 13% HST |
| BC | 12% GST+PST |
| Alberta | 5% GST |
| Quebec | 5% GST + 9.975% QST |

## Who Must Register

- **Required:** Over $30,000/year in revenue
- **Voluntary:** Below threshold, can still register

## Deadlines

- Quarterly filers: Due dates vary
- Annual filers: 6 months after year end

## Digital Products

GST/HST applies to:
- Digital products sold to Canadians
- Services performed in Canada
- Platform fees

This platform helps track and calculate, but consult a CPA for filing.
